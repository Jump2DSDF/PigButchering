- Female persona (typical in pig butchering)

Initial Trust Building Tactics:

- Casual conversation after initial contact

- No immediate pitch or suspicious behavior
- Natural relationship development

Relationship Development:
- Irina began building romantic connection with victim
- Romance element central to this scam
- Emotional manipulation through romantic interest
- Gradual progression from friendship to romance
- Creating emotional dependency before financial discussion

Relationship Timeline:
- Started with wrong number text
- Progressed to regular friendly conversation
- Developed into romantic relationship
- Financial/investment topic introduced after emotional bond established
- Classic pig butchering romance scam progression

Communication Channels:
- Text messages (initial contact)
- Continued text messaging (relationship building)
- Platform websites (birbayap.vip, birbayos.vip)
- Specific platform for file-sharing or instructions not mentioned

Social Engineering Elements:
- Wrong number as non-threatening entry
