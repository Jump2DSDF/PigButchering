CASE 48 - PIG BUTCHERING SCAM ANALYSIS (stdubrye.top)
=====================================

CASE OVERVIEW:
- Primary Platform: stdubrye.top
- Scam Type: Pig Butchering Scam with Fraudulent Crypto Trading Platform
- Victim: California resident
- Total Loss: $240,000+
- Additional Extraction Attempt: $275,000 (withdrawal "fee" demand)
- Platform Status: No longer operational

=====================================
6-STEP CHAIN OF THOUGHT TTP ANALYSIS
=====================================

STEP 1: IDENTIFY THE ATTACK VECTOR
-----------------------------------

Primary Attack Vectors Identified:
1. Online Dating App Recruitment
   - Initial contact made through online dating platform
   - Scammer posed as romantic interest
   - Classic "romance scam" entry point
   - Dating apps provide rich target environment (people seeking connection)

2. Investment Opportunity Introduction
   - After establishing connection, scammer introduced crypto trading
   - Framed as "short-term crypto asset trading"
   - Positioning: sharing profitable opportunity with romantic interest
   - Leverages trust from romantic relationship

3. Platform Direction
   - Victim directed to specific fraudulent platform: stdubrye.top
   - Platform presented as legitimate crypto trading site
   - Scammer guided victim through account opening process
   - Victim instructed to make deposits "at the direction of the individual"

Attack Vector Assessment:
- Initial Contact: Online dating application (unspecified)
- Relationship Type: Romantic interest
- Trust Building: Romantic relationship establishment
- Investment Introduction: "Short-term crypto asset trading" opportunity
- Platform Presentation: stdubrye.top crypto trading platform
- Control Method: Direct trading instructions from scammer

MITRE ATT&CK Mapping:
- T1566.002 - Phishing: Spearphishing Link (dating app messaging)
- T1598.003 - Phishing for Information: Social Media (dating app)
- T1534 - Internal Spearphishing (platform migration from dating app to trading platform)
