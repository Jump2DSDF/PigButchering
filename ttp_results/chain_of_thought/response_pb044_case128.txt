3. ESCALATION PHASE:
   - Larger "opportunities" introduced

   - Funds transferred to fake platform
   - Platform continues showing "profits"

4. EXPLOITATION PHASE:
   - Victim liquidates retirement accounts ($500K)

   - Victim takes personal loans for $12M opportunity
   - Total victim investment reaches $3.8M+

5. EXIT PHASE:

   - All funds stolen
   - No recovery possible

- No explicit "tax" or "fee" barriers mentioned (different from some pig butchering cases)
- Hard exit: Platform simply disappeared

Technical Infrastructure:
