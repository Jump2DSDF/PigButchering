**Reasoning:**

1. **Initial Contact Platform**: Dating app (unspecified which one - could be Tinder, Bumble, Hinge, Match, etc.)
2. **Communication Migration**: WhatsApp - This is a deliberate tactic to move off the dating platform where the scammer could be reported or detected
3. **Lure Type**: This starts as a romance-based lure (meeting on dating app) that evolves into an investment opportunity

The phrase "at some point, Dylan brought up the topic of investing in crypto assets" indicates a gradual transition from romantic/personal conversation to financial topics. This timing is critical - it shows the scammer waited until some level of trust was established before introducing the investment angle.

The victim was convinced to "sell some of their other investments" which demonstrates significant influence and trust had been built. This is a major red flag showing the psychological manipulation was successful.
