### Step 1: Initial Contact Analysis (접근 및 유인)

**Victim 1:**
- Initial contact: "met online" - platform unspecified
- Communication migration: Not explicitly mentioned
- Lure type: Investment opportunity (crypto asset trading)
- Approach: Online acquaintance persuaded victim to deposit into trading account

**Victim 2:**
- Initial contact: "popular dating app" - specific app not named
- Communication migration: YES - moved from dating app to WhatsApp
- Lure type: Romance + investment opportunity (combined approach)
- Approach: Romantic interest suggested investing together for "strong financial future"

**Analysis:** This case demonstrates two distinct attack vectors:
1. Social relationship → investment pitch (Victim 1)
2. Romance scam → investment fraud (Victim 2, classic Pig Butchering)

Both victims were directed to the same fraudulent platform (MIC Markets at micmarkfx.com).

  * Social proof (successful platform claim)

**Victim 2:**
- Scammer persona: Romantic partner from dating app
- Identity deception: Used stolen photos and information for fake dating profile (catfishing)

- Relationship type: Romantic partner with financial partnership angle
- Psychological tactics:
  * Trust building through romance
  * Proof of concept (scammer added $1,000 and showed 25% profit)
  * Social engineering (joint investment for future together)
  * Pressure and bullying (demanded more funding from 401(k) or loans)
  * Reciprocity (scammer promised to pay part of withdrawal fee)
  * Sunk cost exploitation (victim already invested, pushed for more)

**Trust Building Period:**
- Victim 1: Not specified, but "over time" suggests weeks to months
- Victim 2: Extended period - moved from dating app, built romantic relationship, demonstrated trades before soliciting large investments
