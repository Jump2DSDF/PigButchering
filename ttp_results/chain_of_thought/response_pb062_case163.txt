CONCLUSION:
-----------
This case represents a streamlined pig butchering scam that employs professional credentialing rather than romantic relationship building. The use of a "stock broker" persona creates immediate credibility and provides a logical pathway to cryptocurrency trading discussions. The scam demonstrates efficiency in victim acquisition and fund extraction through the fraudulent platform capiturly.io. The exit scam pattern (platform shutdown, withdrawal blocking) is characteristic of mass-scale fraud operations that target multiple victims simultaneously. The use of cryptocurrency ensures irreversibility of transactions and complicates law enforcement recovery efforts. Sharon's role as guide and expert reduces victim skepticism and facilitates the deposit of funds into the scammer-controlled wallet.
